Pizza

A Delicious Deluxe Pizza

IT'S NOT DELIVERY. IT'S DELISSIO!!!

Ingredients

  • Crust (wheat flour, water, canola oil, toasted wheat crumb, modified palm oil, yeast, sugar, salt, flavour, carotene), sauce (water, tomato paste, canola oil, salt, dried garlic, sugar, herb, spices), part skim pizza mozzarella cheese (part skim milk, bacterial culture, salt, calcium chloride, microbial enzyme), pepperoni, cooked italian sausage (soy), red and green bell pepper, onion, black olive, salt.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (205°C)
  2. Remove frozen pizza from all packaging
  3. Place frozen pizza directly on the middle rack of preheated oven. Bake for 17-18 minutes or until cheese is melted and crust is golden brown. Let stand for 5 minutes before serving
